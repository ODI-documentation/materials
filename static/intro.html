ODI: an Ontology of the Destini incrociati by Italo Calvino

ODI is an Ontology of Il castello dei destini incrociati written by Italo Calvino. This project aims to study the narrative use and the relationships between the tarot cards in each story of the work. It was realised as a Master's thesis in Italian Studies at the University of Bologna under the supervision of Professor F. Tomasi and Dr V. Pasqual.
